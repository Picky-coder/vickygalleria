About Me

Hello,

My name is Vimolman Nilsson, and I am an illustrator and Manga and Manhwa translator. Welcome to my portfolio website! I created this space to share my creative vision and showcase my work. I have gained experience in various creative fields, including writing novels, translating manga and manhwa, and working in animation. These experiences have inspired me to explore and develop my artistic skills.

​ I began teaching myself how to draw, starting with sketches and progressing to painting on canvas. With advancements in digital drawing technology, I became intrigued by the idea of bringing my imagined characters to life visually, rather than relying solely on text descriptions. This curiosity led me to study character design, beginning with illustrations for stickers and logos.

​ My designs primarily focus on characters, often accompanied by backstories that give them depth and personality. However, I also enjoy creating standalone illustrations, such as floral compositions and abstract figures, which are often influenced by my mood.
