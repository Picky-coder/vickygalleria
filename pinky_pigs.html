Smiley Pinky Pig Collection

Project type: Character design

Explore the fun and quirky world of Smiley Pinky Pig!
This project was inspired by the anime Seven Deadly Sins. In the story, there is a pig named Hawk, a cute character known for being excellent at eating leftovers, earning him the nickname 'The Leftovers Warrior.' Now, let's take a look at the pigs in my collection!

Smiley Pinky Pig is lazy

This pink pig should look like it's lying down on the sand, but when I added the background, it ended up looking a bit strange. Still, I feel okay with the figure anyway.

Pinky pig is dancing

Actually, this project is meant to feature Pinky pigs in various actions. This collection will showcase dancing, smiley Pinky pigs in different versions.

Another variation of a dancing, smiley Pinky pig.

The pigs look happy and funny. I couldn't help but laugh while sketching them.

An another variation of a dancing, smiley Pinky pig.

I tried to make this pig wear clothes, but it was a bit difficult, so I just added some color instead.

And another variation of a dancing, smiley Pinky pig.
