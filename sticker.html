Stickers

Project type: Graphic Design

I drew characters from translations and depicted them in cute, cartoon-like poses and with various emotions. Let's see which manga or manhwa they are from.

Leoniar is feeling shy

This is my favorite character from the manhwa titled "I Became the Male Lead’s Adopted Daughter." Her name is Leoniar Boleoti, the adopted daughter of Philio Boleoti, the grand duke of the Boleoti duchy. I absolutely adore her for all her cute and endearing reactions, which have been a big inspiration for this project.

Leoniar is thinking

In fact, she has a thoughtful personality because she retains memories from her previous life as an adult. I find her thoughtfulness incredibly endearing and cute.

Leoniar is deep in thought, imagining something wonderful

The manhwa features many scenes where she has moments of realization, like 'Ahhh, I finally figured it out!' I absolutely love it when she shows this reaction.

Leoniar is showing a resigned expression

One of her charms is her ability to accept reality and let it take its course without dwelling on the past.

Oops! Version

Perhaps because she retains memories from her past life as an adult, whenever she does something wrong or is caught off guard, she reacts like this.

Leoniar is trying to hide her wrongdoing with her joy

I love how she often unintentionally behaves in ways a typical six-year-old wouldn’t, due to her memories from her adult life. There are so many delightful scenes where she tries to mask her joy after accidentally doing something uncharacteristic of a child.
