"Lilith" from "The Holy Witch in the Iron Mask"

Project type: Character design

Introducing to one of my work, Character Design. This artwork showcases a collection of unique and original characters inspired by a novel written by myself. Check out my portfolio to see the creative and imaginative work that went into bringing these characters to life.

"Lilith" from "The Holy Witch in the Iron Mask."

This character, named Lilith, is the main protagonist of my novel. She embodies a beauty cursed by the creator god. I designed her to retain a breathtaking appearance that reflects her divine origins—transcendent above humans, yet still bearing a resemblance to humanity.

Lilith in Another Masked Version with angelwings

I actually designed several versions of her wearing a mask, but I believe two of them suit her best in this post. This post is a bit special. The wings remain as a reminder that she is somehow connected to the creation gods.

Lilith in the side post without masked

When I created this image, I started from the concept of reflecting a beauty that sparks human greed and a desire to possess it—one of the key elements driving the conflict in my novel.

Lilith without a mask in another post.

To create this image, I used AI to assist with editing and to maintain the appearance of the face as in the previous image. However, working with AI isn't always easy. To achieve the desired result, I had to spend a lot of time experimenting and making mistakes until I finally got an image and perspective that closely matched what I had envisioned.
